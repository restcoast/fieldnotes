Sertraline
I felt nothing
(was it working)
All was the same as it was before.
But then I felt something,
A bit more than nothing,
but all was the same as it was before.
And then I felt something
like nothing before, 
is this what it’s like
or was there more?

There was more, and more, and more, and more,
I wasn’t the same as I was - 
Before.
Friends were worried
(dunno what for)
So paramedics arrived,
Knocked on my door.

T’was a pleasure, being sectioned,
Ran away two weeks later,
An unforeseen consequence
Of good behaviour.
