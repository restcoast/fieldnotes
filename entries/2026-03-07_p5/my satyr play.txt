My Satyr Play

Clueless
in a hot tub
Zeus thundered a warning
(my way)
Midnight tears at the embassy
Athena laid out the game.
Artemis brought evenings
of ancient ruins
and cats.
Stranded in Athens
Hermes mischief fought back.
Madness crept in
Dionysus has entered the chat,
at Apollo’s Gate
laughing,
“Welcome to the first act.”
