My Satyr Play

Clueless in the hot tub, Zeus thundered warnings my way, 
Midnight tears at the embassy, Athena laid out the game.
Yet calm evenings with Artemis, ancient ruins, and cats.
Stranded in Athens, Hermes mischief fought back.
Madness crept in, Dionysus joined the cast.
Laughter at Apollo’s gate, it was just the first act.
