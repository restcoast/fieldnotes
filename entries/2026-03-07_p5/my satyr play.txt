My Satyr Play

Clueless
in a hot tub,
Zeus thundered a warning my way.
Midnight tears at the embassy,
Athena laid out the game.
Artemis brought evenings
of ancient ruins
and cats.
Stranded in Athens,
Hermes mischief fought back.
Madness crept in, 
Dionysus entered the chat.
At Apollo’s Gate,
laughing,
welcome to the first act.
