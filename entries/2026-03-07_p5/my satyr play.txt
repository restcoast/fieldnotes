My Satyr Play

Clueless in the hot tub,
Zeus thundered warnings my way, 

Midnight tears at the embassy,
Athena laid out the game.

Yet calm evenings with Artemis, 
ancient ruins, and cats.

Stranded in Athens,
Hermes mischief fought back. 

April Fools Day indeed,
Divine laughter cracked,

I joined at Apollo’s Gate,
This was just the first act.
